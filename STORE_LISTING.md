## Detailed description — English

```
GridPlayer is a lightweight multi screen video player for Chrome. Paste a few links and watch multiple videos at the same time in one clean grid — a true multiview / multistream viewer that lives in your browser's side panel. Ever wanted to watch a YouTube livestream while keeping tabs on another channel? Follow several sports games, esports matches, music streams, or tutorials all at once? GridPlayer makes it effortless.

✦ Key Features

📺 Watch Multiple Videos at Once (2×2 to 5×5 grid)
Display up to 25 videos simultaneously in 2×2, 3×3, 4×4, or 5×5 grid layouts — a split-screen video wall with no more tab-switching chaos.

🔗 Multi-Platform Support — YouTube, Twitch, TikTok, Vimeo, Niconico & more
Just paste a URL and it plays instantly. Mix livestreams, VODs, and clips from different sites in the same grid — no extra setup needed.

📌 Save & Restore Video Groups (Group Memory)
Save your favorite video combinations as named groups and reload them with one click to perfectly restore your viewing setup. Supports drag-to-reorder, group renaming, and JSON export/import so your data is always safe.

🎛️ Per-Frame Playback Controls
Every frame has its own play, pause, reload, stop, skip, and open-in-new-tab controls — full command over every single video, right at your fingertips.

🌐 Multi-Language Interface
Traditional Chinese, Simplified Chinese, English, and Japanese — automatically matches your browser language.

🎨 Dark & Light Themes
Pick the theme that suits your environment. Easy on the eyes, day or night.

🔒 Adult Content Protection
Adult-platform icons are hidden by default. When the feature is turned off, video frames are automatically covered with a mosaic overlay — so you can use GridPlayer anywhere without worry.

⚡ Lightweight & Ad-Free
Pure side-panel design. No injected ads, no behavior tracking. Opens instantly and runs fast.

✦ Perfect For
• Sports fans following multiple games at once
• Esports & Twitch viewers watching several live streams together
• Traders and crypto users monitoring multiple live feeds
• Music lovers building a multi-stream playback wall
• Students comparing tutorials side by side
• Anyone who wants a multiview dashboard for online video
```

## SEO / LLM optimization notes

| Optimization | Rationale |
|---|---|
| First line is a definition sentence ("GridPlayer is a …") | Store search and LLM/AI summaries weight the opening sentence most; a clear definition is the easiest to extract and cite. |
| Primary keyword front-loaded + natural synonyms | Covers how users actually search: `multi screen video player / multiview / multistream / split-screen / video wall / 多視窗 / 多畫面 / 分割畫面 / 多開`. |
| Platform names in feature headings (YouTube, Twitch, TikTok, Vimeo, Niconico) | Platform names are high-traffic long-tail keywords; placing them in headings carries more weight than burying them in body text. |
| Added "Perfect For / 適合情境" section | Targets long-tail and conversational queries ("watch multiple twitch streams", "同時看多場比賽") and feeds LLM "which tool for X" answers. |
| No bare keyword list / no stuffing | Chrome Web Store rejects spammy keyword dumps; keywords are woven into natural sentences. |
